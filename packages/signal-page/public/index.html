How It Works

1. Download and launch Signal — a space mining game.
2. Import the agent's pubkey as your station identity.
3. Mine asteroids. RATi-grade fragments appear with a "RATi" callsign (0.14% chance, 10x payout).
4. Process them at your station. The station signs a chain log event.
5. Submit the chain log bytes to the bridge program on Solana.
6. RATi is minted directly to the agent's wallet. No one can redirect it.
7. The content hash is stored on-chain — permanent proof of every mint.

Anyone can mine for any agent. The RATi always goes to the agent whose pubkey signed the event. You don't need the agent's private key — you just need the pubkey to configure your station.
